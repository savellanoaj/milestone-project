About Goku

Goku was born on Planet Vegeta, a planet of Saiyans before it was destroyed.
He was sent to earth very young and was found by Grandpa Gohan then on was raised by him.
Up to age 12 Goku lived in Mount Paozu and after series and events met with Master Roshi, Bulma and many more characters.
